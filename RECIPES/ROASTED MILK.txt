-----------------
|  ROASTED MILK	 |
-----------------

INGREDIENTS:
1 (14 oz.) can of condensed milk
3 eggs
2 cups of milk (whole or 2%)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

PREPARATION:
1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
2. In a small pot, boil sugar and water until it becomes caramel.
3. Pour the hot caramel in the baking dish.
4. In the blender, mix condensed milk, milk and eggs. Pour over the caramelized mold.
5. Bake until firm and brown, 50-60 minutes.
6. Let cool and serve. Keep refrigerated.
