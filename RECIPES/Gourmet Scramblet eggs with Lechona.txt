 -------------------------------------
| GOURMET SCRAMBLED EGGS WITH LECHONA |
 -------------------------------------

INGREDIENTS:

- 2 whole organic white eggs
- 1/4 onion
- 1/2 tomato
- 50g colombian lechona (from tolima)
- salt
- pepper
- rice
	
PREPARATION:

1. Break the two eggs in a hot pan
2. Add the lechona 
3. Add the vegetables
4. Cook 15 min
5. Enjoy
