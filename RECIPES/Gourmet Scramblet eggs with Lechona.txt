
 -----------------------------------
|GOURMET SCRAMBLED EGGS WITH LECHONA|
 -----------------------------------
INGREDIENTS
-1 2 whole organic white eggs
-2 1/4 onion
-3 1/2 tomato
-4 50g colombian lechona (from tolima)
-5 salt
-6 pepper
-7 rice
	
PREPARATION
1. Break the two eggs in a hot pan
2. Add the lechona 
3. Add the vegetables
4. Cook 15 min
5. Enjoy
